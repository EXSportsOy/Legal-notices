Report a Bug

Found something that doesn't work as expected? Let us know — we read every report and use them to improve SurveyTools.

Tap the button below to open a pre-filled email template in your email app. Replace each [placeholder] with your information, then send.

Please include the following:
• Device — make and model (e.g. Samsung Galaxy S23, iPhone 15 Pro)
• OS version — e.g. Android 14 / iOS 17.4
• App version — shown in app settings or on the Google Play store page
• What went wrong — describe the bug in plain language
• Steps to reproduce — what did you tap or enter before it happened?
• Expected vs actual behaviour — what should have happened, and what did instead?
• Severity — minor glitch, broken feature, or crash?

Optional:
• Your email address — if you'd like a follow-up reply from us
• A screenshot — attach it directly to the email before sending
How to take a screenshot:
• Android: Press the Power and Volume Down buttons at the same time
• iPhone: Press the Side button and Volume Up button at the same time

Attach the screenshot to the email before sending — it helps us reproduce the issue much faster.
